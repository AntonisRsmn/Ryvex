
# 🛡️ Ryvex — Moderation You Can Audit, Trust, and Control

**Ryvex** is a **moderation-first Discord bot** built for servers that take  
**staff accountability, transparency, and long-term stability** seriously.

Ryvex is not another bloated “all-in-one” bot.  
It is a **structured moderation platform** designed to make every action  
**clear, traceable, and fair** — for both members *and* moderators.

If moderation matters in your server, Ryvex was built for you.

Ryvex is designed for:

## 🧾 Case-Based Moderation — Transparency First

Every moderation action creates a **persistent case**:

- ⚠️ Warnings
- ⏳ Timeouts
- 👢 Kicks
- 🔨 Bans & unbans
- 🤖 AutoMod punishments
- 🔒 Channel & role enforcement

Each case records:
- Who acted
- Who was affected
- Why it happened
- When it happened
- Duration (when applicable)

Nothing is hidden. Nothing is overwritten.  
Perfect for **staff accountability, appeals, and audits**.

## 🤖 Smart AutoMod — Predictable, Not Random

Ryvex includes a **fully configurable AutoMod system** that protects your server from:

- 🚫 Spam
- 🔗 Unauthorized links
- 🤬 Offensive language (built-in + custom lists)

### What makes Ryvex AutoMod different?

- Unified warning counter across **all AutoMod triggers**
- **Escalating punishments that don’t reset**
- Quick presets: **Soft / Medium / Strict**
- Channel & role bypass support
- Optional **warn-only mode**

Members always know **where they stand**.  
Moderators always stay **in control**.

## 🎨 Rich Interactive Dashboards

Every informational command uses **multi-page interactive embeds** with ◀ ▶ navigation:

- `/botinfo` — 3 pages: Overview, Live Stats, Links & Info
- `/userinfo` — 3 pages: Identity, Moderation Snapshot, Leveling
- `/staff` — 3 pages: Overview, Leaderboard, Recent Activity
- `/changelog` — Timeline overview + per-release pages with stats
- `/setup` — 17-page interactive guide
- `/settings` — 10-page interactive dashboard
- `/rules` — Paginated display (3 per page)
- `/history` commands — Enriched entries with reason, timestamp, and duration

No clutter. No walls of text. Clean, navigable information.
